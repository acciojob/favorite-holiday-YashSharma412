Tithal Beach


A place to enjoy, contemplate, shoot pictures, eat sweet corns and drink fresh coconut water watching the sun set in the horizon at the Tithal beach,is really enchanting. Worth a stroll to witness the hues of sunset.... A romantic experience!!!

Following are the top 5 attractions of Tithal.

  1. Very close to the main city of Valsad.
  2. The BAPS Swaminarayn Temple on the coastline is a beautiful religious place to visit.
  3. The Sunset at the Beach is a sight to behold
  4. The everlasting cold breeze is a remedy to your stressed mind.
  5. A beach that is appreciated by young and old alike.

Must-try foods when you are at tithal.

  • The fresh Sweet Corn, roasted and/or boiled makes for a light snack.
  • Its mandatory to drink Nariyal Pani (CocoNut Water) when at the beach.
  • Peanuts and berries boiled in haldi water is a snack to try.
  • Try the Dabeli with Tea, a treat to go along with the sunset.
  • If you have a bigger apetite, the trusty Masala Maggi stalls will never disapoint.
